

## User Manual

###### Computer Science Schedule Generating System

------

### I am a user

###### Step 1: Open the Home page in browser and choose "I am a student"

 ![img](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/r1EmNfkEWcLNZvoZnXZOS-mGMvMdHoVNfTH4EnDQh8SaFrlaS7Or_W-gMP1L39kS8xRMvCJodt2TUyj0-dir9RVv09cTMEzYK-ub_OTp8UIi7zArecAGei8o5sTpVveAkBJ2huje)

###### Step 2:  If you are a new user, click "Register"; if you are an existing user, enter your username and password, then click "Login".

 ![img](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/osGZUoZL_bjvi7Lu4WvGRBCGX7QKFe7w50PCk4txqgwgd7gi7hfd0IQ3FNkX4GGfdpxklPXUj0k741F-WR3eSiXCdpHp30UUxj5zBOx2tp0oWc5H2eWiYt2wctRsz9suIxPbIXl9)

###### Step 3 (New User Only): Create your new account by entering your username and password desired, then click "Register".  Then click "Return to Login Page" and follow the step in step 2 for existing users.

![img](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/ZnJpSuiHw1PH9f5G7o7_OvpkW8Qq5Wy2zkgBGhifrIDbUp33Qp7YpypJa0PL9xnnq3ipHx703BeNTnRi6FL8bBQQcZGNAPvy8I-Td01G_4PrWTObVjXGZ4fpCozIn__2ozLeT8xG)

###### Step 4: If you never enter your course taken and track information, please click "select track and course taken" to enter your academic information. If you have already finished the step, please click "Generate Schedule" to create your course schedule for next semester.

 ![img](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/efghh6ryJa-LmM7qKPAQ0O58gLrwplvNwLiTACoKDsrPEMkyfm-0zEBSu8sO5GjJpw7WwnX3OVIHUPNfkk3WhuYuDLHF4SKmtDD2zHZyNCeCCAiyEuqqV9N0NXmmAssoct141yA3)

###### Step 5 (New User Only): Select your track by selecting the corresponding button, then select the courses you have already taken by selecting the corresponding buttons. After entering information, click "Update", and then click "Return" to return the page in step 4. Follow the instruction in step 4 for existing user to generate schedule.

 ![img](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/rh5-Q77mho5oW_rXSTo1tZ-9CFEqUx-0EAFx_zmynG0BSPEVZ5WycZRzmnPx9MAeJBg-KZJ-kYabxrs8rHkv0JLkyQyQZzISjfXCL2N-i7txZNMFMJdh7spg3tbkgnMlDRYsNSVI)

###### Step 6: Select one of your favorite auto-generated schedule that contains courses in General, Core, Breadth, Depth requirements by selecting the corresponding button. Then click "Submit" to select the technical elective course you want to take.

![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/YwlJdxIaocZTtgGOiShS6_Ant_e5Gs6wpOcrIEd9b_kLt9OLcIeYKa58kCBw17JRGMCPqlRH2-_Mnk-mcO-WLj0d22GRFx0-rH0sBAvA3EQIy4dAtt4G1z_C4OUvycFXzhdqc7uM)

###### Step 7: Select one of your favorite technical elective course (or select none of them if you are satisfied with the auto-generated schedule). Click "Submit" to generate and get a view of the final schedule.

 ![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/PEOBnrfcXM-8KKWmAzPjP9ARz7EWf_shD9FPZwYjL0FEeGxpoKDgiYoz6U_t8E5k7ueOJWVSrvwx7NoJBC17RDNGhuhYsQpiWHSPbKhcjjOB-fPxH6mNsq03OZtOx0aaIrmag377) 

###### Step 8: Get the final schedule. Take a screenshot and proceed to sis system to select the courses.

![img](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/kW1lsZ_WOGL7T0uYcG-vW-8FqxOFhBO8Py9l4uT-nwiMv30BNgiX77MZPWN-LZexXb1d1tjdHkFWXnphG0l9h08ZeUiNrqfJwyWF7WpsJm-DG0glXEroG1zB-XP8qAWBWBHlVjan)

### I am an administrator

###### Step 1: Open the Home page in browser and choose "I am an administrator"

![img](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/r1EmNfkEWcLNZvoZnXZOS-mGMvMdHoVNfTH4EnDQh8SaFrlaS7Or_W-gMP1L39kS8xRMvCJodt2TUyj0-dir9RVv09cTMEzYK-ub_OTp8UIi7zArecAGei8o5sTpVveAkBJ2huje)

###### Step 2: Login in by entering the provided administrator username and password, and then click "Login".

![img](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/SWprGVpUdEeh6Cb6CvPkLPB0KVxprgNVzv_vOgixKXHISehILxdM4QA5oHEylXe1M0_OuTZle-lv0uYtvQT_ie3u8fkAecNbjLcbaIzIhYgWsJauXRSj7mahB3dUSIqlZnwkp9rs)

###### Step 3: Click "Course Manage" to create new courses, view the existing course information, update the current courses information, and remove the existing courses.

![img](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/jN-lIiyqgSRD7cQWHnqecgvVRRWS1wIvf8t9she-IkXmlsh13N8GcbRFTkxQY3L53SHtx_sAyqT9Ne_EZBJlxiC7naXvTiOOtmuGJTvSyF_Su0FKnlo7e3gmBndA8A4EE8Oldaop)

###### Step 4:  If you want to create new courses, click "Add Course". If you want to view the existing course information, please view this page. If you want to update the current courses information or remove the existing courses, please click "update" or "delete" button next to the corresponding course row.

![img](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/jZtDYyrhczBvaVU45nb7vtshP_al9kr-qMPj8xL5LmU-ZkrL3g6U17hxD3aD07dNhxB5cwAYxpVJiR8hwOYi57FZnspbzIZcboTxn5fRObLJfI1D7J5o3s92MvOkYzDvG0tEVvG5)

###### Step 5: Follow the instruction on the right to create new courses or update the current courses information. Then click "add course" or "update course" to submit the change.

![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-VknNtephrn_kGUVx5gGiApo5LuqcTZJg0t62jZeqaynsrIXgqrHGhlAw7hBdbjANhmSu1ictAlqi95nYp73IVXVVr7gXG_VxD618fQ_uy2Kv-fHs4C4XyDABIFAEtMy3JKMchqP)
